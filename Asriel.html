ASRIEL

BIO
Years active: 2006-2015
Genres: Gothic metal, Dark ambient
Members: KOKOMI, Kurose Keisuke

Asriel, pronounced as "asu-rie-ru," is a Japanese goth rock band known for their poetic lyrics, addictive melodies, and lead singer Kokomi's alluring voice. They have had remarkable growth over their short career and have been my top favorite artist, along with Kokomi, for the past 6 years.
(says the creator of the website lol)

There have been quite a few albums including ABYSS (2013), Resurrection (2014), and the Ragnarok box set containing all the remaining 144 tracks, excluding their 2 main albums. They have also been featured on the opening of the visual novel 11eyes. (Written by MANEYAC)

Asriel's activities started with participation in Comiket and the Music Media-mix Market (M3) events, where they sold dōjinshi music albums. In April 2008, Asriel composed the song "Metamorphose" for the anime adaptation of Monochrome Factor. This song was released as a maxi single and was their first work produced by a major label.
